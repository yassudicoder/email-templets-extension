    Frequently asked questions

    Is it free?

    Yes — completely free, with every feature unlocked (unlimited templates, variables, images, HTML-email templates). There's no paywall, and nothing is ever gated behind payment.

    Do you read or send my emails?

    No. Your templates stay on your device, and the extension never reads or transmits your email content. There's no account, and any anonymous usage analytics are off by default and opt-in only.

    Which sites does it work on?

    Gmail (mail.google.com) and LinkedIn (linkedin.com) — new messages, replies, inline replies, and comments.

    Where is my data stored, and what if I uninstall?

    Everything is stored locally in your browser. You can export your templates to a JSON file at any time as a backup, and re-import them after a reinstall.

    Can I use my own keyboard shortcut?

    Yes. The default is Alt+A, and you can change it in the settings.

    What languages does it support?

    The extension's interface is available in English, Spanish, Portuguese (Brazil), German, French, Hindi, and Japanese. It automatically uses your Chrome language — no setting needed. Your templates themselves are always whatever language you write them in.
